Hotel Highlights

Our Legacy

Founded in 2005, Silver Sands Hotel began as a small boutique hotel with a big dream – to redefine hospitality in Tamil Nadu. Over the years, we have grown into a luxurious retreat for travelers worldwide. With a focus on comfort, elegance, and warm service, our journey has been one of dedication, continuous improvement, and heartfelt guest experiences.

Special Deals

We believe in making your stay more rewarding. Enjoy seasonal discounts, early bird packages, festive staycations, and honeymoon specials. Our current offer includes up to 30% off for bookings made 15 days in advance and complimentary breakfast with every suite booking.

Luxury Accommodation

Choose from a variety of accommodations designed to fit your needs. Whether it's a cozy Single Room, a spacious Double Room, or a luxurious Deluxe Suite with modern amenities and stunning views — we ensure every room is a haven of comfort and style.

Top-Class Amenities

Silver Sands Hotel offers a range of world-class facilities including free high-speed Wi-Fi, a rooftop swimming pool, an in-house spa, 24/7 room service, a fitness center, a multi-cuisine restaurant, business conference halls, and a private airport shuttle service.
