Our Facilities

Experience unmatched convenience and comfort through our wide range of facilities tailored for business, leisure, and family guests.

Infinity Pool

Take a dip in our rooftop infinity pool, surrounded by scenic views and calm breezes — the perfect escape after a long day of travel or meetings.

Wellness Spa

Relax, recharge, and rejuvenate in our luxurious spa with a variety of therapies, including ayurvedic treatments, deep tissue massages, and facials.

Stay fit during your travels with our 24/7 gym, equipped with modern cardio and strength-training equipment for all fitness levels.

Conference & Banquet Halls

Host meetings, seminars, or grand celebrations in our fully-equipped halls with state-of-the-art AV systems, seating flexibility, and professional staff support.
